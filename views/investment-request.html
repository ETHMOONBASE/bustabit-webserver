Invest/Divest Request

Warning: Your invest/divest requests aren't processed right away there will be a delay of 1 to 20 games.
Warning: You cannot divest till it's been 24 hours since your last investment.
Note: When investing, you get a % share of the house bankroll. The profit is then divided proportionately to the % share of the house bankroll you own.
Note: When divesting, since the requests aren't processed right away, it may happen that the desired amount to divest isn't available in your investment anymore due to wins on the side of the players. If this happen, your request will be canceled and the amount of the request replaced with 0. We recommend to not divest everything all at once.
